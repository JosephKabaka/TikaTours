FAQs.

What services does your travel agency offer?

We offer a wide range of services, including flight bookings, hotel reservations, guided tours, and car rentals. We also provide customized travel packages to fit your preferences and budget.

How do I book a trip with your agency?

You can book a trip by browsing our available destinations on our website. Once you select a destination, click the “Book Now” button, fill out the booking form, and submit it. We will confirm your booking via email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via credit/debit cards, mobile money (M-Pesa), and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided after booking confirmation.

Can I customize my travel package?

Yes! We offer flexible travel packages. You can reach out to our team to customize your itinerary based on your preferences and budget.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 7 days before the departure date for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 7 days prior may incur a fee. Please refer to our detailed cancellation policy on the terms and conditions page

Are there any group discounts available?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 2(couples) or more people. Contact us for more information about group pricing and special offers.
